Trampette

A simple desert! Family recipe.

A very simple desert/treat! This is a family recipe, I cannot find it anywhere on the net. So simple, yet so good. I recommend you give it a try! It's simply brown sugar on sliced bread, and soaking it in milk! My grandfather always called it a 'Trampette", roughly translating to "Soaked" referring to the bread and sugar being soaked in milk. Deceptively good. I hope you enjoy!

Ingredients

  • Sliced Bread
  • Milk
  • Brown Sugar

Is this necessary?

  1. Put your bread on a plate.
  2. Put your brown sugar on your bread.
  3. Slowly pour a light amount of milk.
  4. Try to get all the brown sugar wet.
  5. Enjoy with a fork!

I hope you enjoy it and don't get diabetes! I sure do, and I'm still diabetes free!
